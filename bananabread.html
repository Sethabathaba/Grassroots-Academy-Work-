Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup sugar
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla essence
  • 1 tablespoon butter/margarine
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/4 milk
  • 3/4 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt

Method

  1. Beat 1 cup of sugar and three eggs together for about 3 to 4 minutes
  2. Add 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  3. Mash 4 ripe Bananas and add to the sugar and egg mixture
  4. Add 1/2 cup oil and 1 tablespoon butter or margarine
  5. Add the eggs and mix until integrate
  6. Add the hot milk and bring everything back to the low heat, stirring continuously until it comes to a boil.
  7. Add 1/4 cup milk in a separate bowl sift 2 cups of flour,
  8. 2 teaspoon baking powder and 1 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
  9. Add to the egg mixture
  10. Prepare 2 baking pans or one big one
  11. Bake in preheated oven at 180 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
